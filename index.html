About Us

Online Tutor has helped hundreds of candidates, worldwide, achieve their dreams of getting into top MBA and Undergraduate programs. We specialize in helping Indian applicants gain admission to the top-20 US and International programs. Our MBA candidates have a 97% success rate to top schools and 100% of our Undergraduate candidates get into one of their top four choices. Our candidates secure scholarships ranging from $10,000 – $125,000. Read more Online Tutor's success stories.

Our counselors and advisors are seasoned top business school and college graduates with a wealth of admissions and industry-specific experience in both the US and Indian markets. We have been in your shoes and understand the unique challenges Indians face in the applications process. Our mission is to help you overcome those obstacles, harness your potential, and help you gain admission to a top-20 MBA or Undergraduate program.
